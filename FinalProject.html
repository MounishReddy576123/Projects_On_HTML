During 1984 I turned eight years old, so I could write well enough to keep a diary for the whole year, but I have to say, the topics I chose to write about were mostly a bit questionable – there was a lot of space devoted to how my tomato plants were growing and which pieces I was playing on the piano (with rather boastful claims about my piano playing ability too, I must admit!). But the best part was my (rather short!) travel journal about a family trip to Kalbarri, Western Australia – a coastal town about six hours’ north of Perth.
